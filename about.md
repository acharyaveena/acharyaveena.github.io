I'm Veena Acharya, a Technical Writer with a strong software development background. With **10 years of experience as a Software Engineer**, I bring deep technical expertise to every documentation project. Over the last **2 years**, I transitioned into technical writing, combining my hands-on development experience with a passion for creating clear, developer-focused documentation.

---

## My Approach

1. **Discover** — Understand the product, audience, and goals
2. **Plan** — Design information architecture aligned with user journeys
3. **Write** — Draft content using minimalist principles and plain language
4. **Review** — Collaborate with SMEs for technical accuracy
5. **Test** — Validate documentation with real users when possible
6. **Publish** — Deliver polished, accessible content

---

## Why I Write

After a decade of building software, I realized that great code means nothing without great documentation. I've been the developer frustrated by missing docs, and that drives my work today — writing the documentation I wish I'd had.

My engineering background gives me a unique edge: I can read the code, understand the architecture, and write for the developer who needs to ship.
